# Overview

Fancl Corporation is a Japanese health and beauty company founded in 1980 by Mr. Yoshinori Kobayashi. The company is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, and has over 1,000 employees. Fancl specializes in the production of health supplements and beauty products, and also provides health and beauty consulting services.
